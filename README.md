# 🟠 XBTC

## Digital Scarcity Reimagined

**XBTC** is a next-generation digital asset inspired by Bitcoin’s core philosophy of scarcity, decentralization, and trust — redesigned for modern Web3 infrastructure, scalability, and real-world utility.

---

## 🌐 Introduction

In the evolving landscape of digital finance, Bitcoin introduced the concept of decentralized scarcity. However, modern blockchain ecosystems demand more — flexibility, interoperability, and utility.

**XBTC bridges this gap.**

It preserves the fundamental principles of Bitcoin while enabling:
- Seamless integration into DeFi ecosystems
- Cross-chain operability
- Scalable infrastructure
- Advanced smart contract functionality
