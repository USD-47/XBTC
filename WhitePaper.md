# 📖 Executive Summary

XBTC is a next-generation digital asset designed to combine **Bitcoin’s scarcity model** with the **flexibility and scalability of modern blockchain ecosystems**.

While Bitcoin established the foundation of decentralized value, it lacks native adaptability to DeFi, cross-chain ecosystems, and programmable financial infrastructure.

**XBTC solves this limitation by preserving scarcity while enabling utility.**

# 🌍 1. Introduction

The emergence of Bitcoin introduced a new paradigm:  
a decentralized, immutable, and scarce digital asset.

However, as blockchain ecosystems evolved, several limitations became evident:

- Limited programmability
- Lack of DeFi integration
- Scalability constraints
- Minimal interoperability

XBTC is built to **extend Bitcoin’s philosophy into a new era of Web3.**
